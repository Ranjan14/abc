What is coronavirus?

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus.

Most people who fall sick with COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment. However, some will become seriously ill and require medical attention.

preventions

Wear a mask

ear a well-fitting three-layer mask, especially when you can’t physically distance, or if you’re indoors. Clean your hands before putting on and taking off a mask.

Wash your hand

Frequently clean your hands with soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub. If you can, carry alcohol-based rub with you and use it often.

Get vaccinated

When it’s your turn, get vaccinated. Follow local guidance and recommendations about vaccination.

Stop shaking hands

Avoid touching other people. Skin-to-skin contact can transmit covid 19 from one person to another.

Self-quarantine if sick

Call your doctor if you have any symptoms. Stay home until you recover. Avoid sitting, sleeping, or eating with your loved ones even if you live in the same home.

Keep your distance

Stay at least 1 metre away from others, even if they don’t appear to be sick, since people can have the virus without having symptoms.
